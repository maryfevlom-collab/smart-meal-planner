About Smart Meal Planner

Transforming how people cook, eat, and share meals through AI-powered recipe generation

Our Mission

At Smart Meal Planner, we believe that everyone should have access to delicious, affordable meals regardless of their cooking experience or budget. Our AI-powered platform helps you create amazing recipes from ingredients you already have, reducing food waste and saving you money while expanding your culinary horizons.

Our Story

Smart Meal Planner was born from a simple observation: people often have ingredients in their kitchens but struggle to create delicious meals from them. Combined with the challenge of staying within a food budget, this inspired us to develop a solution that would make meal planning accessible to everyone.

Launched in 2023, our platform has grown to serve thousands of users across Ghana and beyond, helping them create over 50,000 recipes and save on their food budgets. Our AI technology continues to learn and improve, offering increasingly personalized recipe suggestions.

Today, we're proud to be at the intersection of culinary tradition and technological innovation, helping people discover the joy of cooking while making practical decisions about their food consumption and budget.
